Safety & Security

Yes, safety is our first priority. We've built multiple layers of protection specifically with women's safety in mind:

  • CNIC Verification Option: Users can verify their identity using their national ID card (CNIC) and get a verified badge on their profile
  • Verified Badges: See who's verified at a glance. You can choose to only connect with verified users - it's your choice
  • Rating & Review System: Read reviews and ratings from other users before deciding to join any activity
  • Report & Block: Instantly report or block anyone who makes you uncomfortable
  • Activity-Only Focus: This is NOT a dating app. Any romantic or inappropriate content is filtered and results in immediate account removal

Users can verify themselves using their CNIC (National Identity Card). Here's how it works:

  • Upload a photo of your CNIC
  • Our system verifies your identity
  • Once verified, you get a "Verified" badge displayed on your profile
  • Unverified users won't have the badge, so everyone can see verification status
  • You can choose who to connect with based on their verification status

The choice is yours - connect with verified users for added confidence, or give newcomers a chance.

We have a Location Verification & Strike System to prevent no-shows:

  • At meetup time, we verify if users actually went to the location
  • If someone doesn't show up without canceling, they get a strike
  • After 3 strikes: Account restrictions apply (limited posting, reduced visibility)
  • No-show history is visible on profiles, so you can see who's reliable
  • This keeps the community accountable and respectful of everyone's time

Yes, absolutely. We have zero tolerance for inappropriate behavior:

  • Report Button: Report any user or activity immediately
  • Block Feature: Block anyone and they won't be able to see or contact you
  • Content Filtering: Posts with inappropriate language, nudity, or romantic content are automatically filtered and blocked
  • Immediate Action: Reported accounts are reviewed within 24 hours, and violations result in immediate removal
  • Our team actively monitors the platform to keep it safe and activity-focused

Every profile gives you the information you need to make an informed decision:

  • Verification Status: See if they're verified with CNIC
  • Ratings & Reviews: Read what other users say about them
  • Activity History: See their past activities and attendance record
  • No-Show History: Check if they have any strikes for not showing up
  • Profile Completeness: Complete profiles with photos and bio indicate serious users

You're always in control of who you choose to connect with.

About Spotmate

No, Spotmate is NOT a dating app.

Spotmate is exclusively for finding activity partners - sports, fitness, hobbies, and recreation. Here's how we enforce this:

  • Activities must be sports, fitness, or hobby-related
  • Romantic or dating-related posts are automatically filtered and blocked
  • Users posting inappropriate content get immediate account removal
  • Our AI and moderation team actively monitor to keep the platform focused on activities

If you're looking for a tennis partner, gym buddy, or someone to go hiking with - that's what Spotmate is for.

Spotmate is completely free. No charges, no subscriptions, no hidden fees.

We believe everyone should have access to finding activity partners without financial barriers. All features are free to use:

  • Post unlimited activities
  • Join as many activities as you want
  • Message other users freely
  • Access all safety features
  • View all profiles and reviews

Our mission is to get people active and connected, not to charge them for it.

Privacy & Data

Your privacy is important to us. Here's how we handle your data:

  • We never sell your data to third parties or advertisers
  • Your CNIC is encrypted and only used for verification
  • Your contact information is never shared with other users unless you choose to share it
  • You control what information appears on your profile
  • You can delete your account and all data at any time

We only use your information to provide the service and keep the platform safe.
